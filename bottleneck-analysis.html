Lead Doc

Three Bottlenecks I Found Inside Your Funnel - Nick Schiffer

All three install in less than 7 days from kickoff. Built to compound. Order by impact.

Snapshot

  • 237K IG @nsbuilders + YouTube @NSBuilders + Modern Craftsman podcast + NS Builders luxury GC credibility since 2014
  • NS Builders = local service business (Eastern MA) with no productised offer for other builders
  • Modern Craftsman podcast = trust vehicle reaching custom builders nationwide, no paid tier
  • 237K IG audience consuming builder-education content with zero conversion path to any product
  • No course, no coaching, no scorecard, no email list attached to the content brand

Bottleneck #1 - 237K Builders Watch the Process, Then Have Nowhere to Buy In

The asymmetry: @nsbuilders has 237,000 followers watching Nick document the NS Builders process - job-site walkthroughs, custom home design, project management, material selection. They trust the craft. They want to build the way Nick builds. And right now there is no product they can buy to get closer to that. The audience watches, nods, and scrolls on.

A $497-$997 "Custom Builder's Playbook" course - translating the NS Builders process into installable systems for $1M-$5M custom residential operators - gives the 237K a product to buy. The course covers: client acquisition without underbidding, project management without Friday firefighting, subcontractor relationships that hold, the estimating process that protects margin, and the Modern Craftsman positioning that separates a quality builder from a commodity GC.

Why it's costing him: - 0.5-1% of a warm 237K IG audience converting to a $497 course at the right offer = 1,200-2,400 buyers Year-1 - Zero friction: the audience already trusts Nick from the content - the sale is warm - The podcast listener is even warmer - they've spent 30-60 minutes per episode with Nick's thinking already

The fix: - "Custom Builder's Playbook" - 6-module self-paced course - Modules: client systems, estimating for margin, project management cadence, sub relationships, Modern Craftsman brand positioning, scale-or-stay decision framework - Includes monthly live Q&A with Nick (60 min) - $497 standard, $997 with personal video review of buyer's custom build business

Estimated impact: Year-1 floor $150-400K at 300-800 buyers x $497-$997.

Why this is #1: Largest warm audience to convert. Highest ceiling. The podcast listenership compounds the IG audience - both channels are pre-heated.

Bottleneck #2 - No Email Capture Between 237K IG Followers and Any Offer

The leak: The @nsbuilders audience has nowhere to give their email. There is no scorecard, no quiz, no audit, no PDF, no opt-in of any kind visible across nsbuilders.com, the IG profile, or the YouTube channel. 237K followers generate reach, but zero compounding asset - because there is no email list.

A "Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard" quiz - 8 questions, returns a 0-100 score, captures email, segments by business stage and biggest operations bottleneck - converts the casual IG follower into a named, tracked email subscriber Nick can market to for life.

Why it's costing him: - 8-12% IG-to-email conversion with the right quiz = 19,000-28,000 incremental email subscribers from existing audience alone - Without segmentation, Nick cannot separate "$1M+ custom builders ready for coaching" from "$300K GCs ready for a course" from "fans and aspiring builders" - No email list = no pre-sell of course launch, no cohort fill, no podcast episode promotion with a conversion event attached

The fix: - "The Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard" - 8-question quiz on ScoreApp or Typeform - Captures email, returns custom PDF action plan based on score band - Segments by: annual build volume, team size, biggest operations gap, interest in group coaching - Triggers 8-touch nurture sequence routed by segment (course pool vs coaching-ready)

Estimated impact: Year-1 lift $50-150K incremental from email-driven course sales + cohort fill. Compounds for life of the list.

Why this is #2: Infrastructure for everything else. Fast install, less than 7 days. The email list is the asset every other bottleneck rides on.

Bottleneck #3 - Modern Craftsman Podcast Has No Paid Tier

The contradiction: Modern Craftsman is one of the most trusted podcasts in custom residential construction. Listeners spend 30-60 minutes per episode with Nick's thinking. That is premium attention from the exact audience who would buy a $2K-$5K group coaching cohort. And right now the podcast generates zero direct revenue beyond brand equity.

A "Modern Craftsman Mastermind" - $2,500-$5,000/yr group coaching cohort for serious custom builders, capped at 40 operators, quarterly live sessions with Nick plus a private community - turns the podcast's most committed listeners into paying clients.

Why it's costing him: - Podcast listeners are the highest-trust, highest-LTV segment of the audience - A 40-person cohort at $3K/yr = $120K ARR that does not currently exist - The cohort also feeds the course ecosystem: prospects who aren't cohort-ready buy the $497-$997 course first, qualify up, then apply for the mastermind - Modern Craftsman is an established brand name - the mastermind runs under it, not under the NS Builders construction company name

The fix: - "Modern Craftsman Mastermind" - quarterly cohorts, 40-person cap - Four live 90-minute sessions per year with Nick - Private community (Skool or Circle) with async Q&A - Application-based: only $1M+ custom residential operators who've read the Playbook or listened to the podcast - $2,500-$5,000/yr depending on cohort tier

Estimated impact: Year-1 floor $100-200K ARR. Compounds with each cohort added.

Why this is #3: Higher setup overhead than #1 or #2, but the highest LTV per client in the stack. Becomes the flagship after the course builds the warm feeder pool.
