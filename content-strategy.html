Phase 3

Content Strategy - Nick Schiffer

Phase 3 deliverable · Hook bank, podcast pivot brief, short-form roadmap, content-to-conversion architecture.

Premise

Nick's 237K @nsbuilders audience is engaged on process and craft. The content mixes job-site walkthroughs, design decisions, material sourcing, and project management reality. None of it routes the viewer to a next step that earns Nick revenue from other builders. Phase 3 builds a content layer with one job: get the right builder onto the Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard.

Eight categories of builder-operator pain hooks. Each pulls a different segment of the 237K toward the scorecard.

  5. How I price custom homes so the client expects to pay more, not less.

Podcast Pivot Brief

Current state: Modern Craftsman podcast is an established trade-education vehicle. Episodes feature Nick's process, guest builders, and craft-focused conversations. No mid-roll or end-roll CTA driving to a product, scorecard, or email capture.

Phase 3 move: add a consistent conversion event to every episode: - Mid-roll (minute 15-20): brief CTA to the Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard - "takes 4 minutes, sends you a custom action plan" - End-roll: CTA to the Custom Builder Mentorship landing page - Episode descriptions: scorecard link + mentorship link as first links in every description

Back-catalog integration: add scorecard + mentorship links to top-50 episode descriptions. These episodes are still getting new listens - add the conversion event retroactively.

New content format to layer in: 10-15 minute "Operator's Cut" solo episodes - Nick working through one specific operations problem from a recent NS Builders project. Structured as: problem, how most builders handle it, how NS Builders handles it, what the data showed. These episodes convert at higher rates because they deliver actionable takeaways in under 20 minutes.

Short-Form Roadmap (90 days)

Weeks 1-3: post 5 hooks/week from Categories 1-2 (estimating, client acquisition) - the highest-pain business triggers for $1M+ custom builders

Weeks 4-6: post 5/week from Categories 3-4 (project management, sub relationships) - operations depth that builds credibility for the mentorship

Weeks 7-9: post 5/week from Categories 5-6 (business operations, Modern Craftsman positioning) - brand differentiation content that primes the mastermind offer

Weeks 10-12: post 5/week from Categories 7-8 (craft and standards, scale and future) + retargeting ads layered on top of organic

Cadence: 5 IG reels + 5 YT shorts + 1 long-form / week. Repurpose ratio 1:5 - one filmed job-site asset = one long-form episode + 5 short cuts.

Conversion Goal

Every piece of content has the same final CTA: "Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard - 4 minutes, custom action plan, in your inbox" - with the link in bio routing to the scorecard quiz. Course launch CTA layers in at week 8 when the email list has 5,000+ subscribers.
