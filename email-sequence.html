Phase 1+2

Email Sequences - Nick Schiffer

Phase 1 + 2 deliverable · Welcome flow (8 touches), pre-call nurture (3 touches), post-call (4 touches over 14 days).

Sequence 1 - Welcome Flow (10 days, 8 touches)

Triggered when a new subscriber finishes the Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard. Segmented by score tier (0-40 / 41-70 / 71-100).

T+0 - Immediate (Score Result)

Subject: Your Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard - score [X/100] + action plan inside

Body: Score reveal, 1-paragraph context on what the score means for a custom build business at their stage, 3-bullet action plan, link to download printable PDF, soft CTA toward the Custom Builder Mentorship.

Goal: deliver promise, set expectations.

T+1 day - The Bridge

Subject: What most custom builders get wrong about their first $2M year

Body: A specific story from an NS Builders project - the operations lesson that lived behind a problem most $1M-$2M custom builders run into. Closes with "the Custom Builder Mentorship is the install version of what fixed this."

Goal: bridge content follower to mentorship applicant.

T+3 days - The Estimating Story

Subject: The estimating line I stopped forgetting (it was costing us $40K a job)

Body: Specific margin-leak story tied to a real NS Builders project. Cost-build sheet teaser. CTA to Custom Builder Mentorship landing.

Goal: prove operational depth behind the content brand.

T+5 days - The Sub Relationship Story

Subject: Why I lost a great framer in year two - and the pay structure that fixed it

Body: The retention conversation most GCs don't have until it's too late. Vulnerable framing. CTA to mentorship landing.

Goal: build trust through admitted operational mistakes.

T+7 days - The Process Bridge

Subject: The system behind the content - what @nsbuilders doesn't show

Body: What it took to build NS Builders into a repeatable process, not just a quality reputation. The mentorship is the install version. CTA to mentorship.

Goal: route content followers into the buying frame.

T+9 days - Soft Pitch Open

Subject: Quick question before I leave your inbox alone

Body: Two-line check-in. "Is the Playbook a fit for where your build business is right now?" Link to landing. "Or hit reply and tell me what's actually broken in your operations."

Goal: segment hot vs warm; route hot to landing, warm to a 1:1 reply for triage.

T+10 days - Last Touch (segmented)

  • Score 71-100 (Mastermind-ready): "You don't need the Playbook first. You need the Modern Craftsman Mastermind application." Routes to mastermind waitlist.
  • Score 41-70 (Mentorship pool): Last call for mentorship application. CTA to mentorship landing.
  • Score 0-40 (Foundations): "Start with the fundamentals. Here's the first one - free." Free resource from the podcast back-catalog + scorecard re-take note in 6 months.

Sequence 2 - Pre-Call Nurture (3 touches before strategy call)

Triggered when a mentorship participant books an optional 1:1 strategy add-on, or when a mastermind applicant books their screening call.

T-7 days

Subject: Before our call - 3 questions I'll ask you

Body: The three questions Nick will ask, in advance. Helps the caller arrive with actual project data, not vague answers.

T-3 days

Subject: What I learned coaching builders last year

Body: Story-driven. One specific insight from a real operator conversation. Sets the context for a useful call.

T-1 day

Subject: Tomorrow at [time] - one thing to bring

Body: Calendar reminder, one specific prep ask (current project count, margin on last two jobs, team headcount).

Sequence 3 - Post-Call Follow-Up (4 touches over 14 days)

Triggered after the strategy call regardless of outcome.

T+1 hour - Thank You + Recap

Subject: Recap from our call + 2 next steps

Body: 1-paragraph recap, 2 specific action items committed to on the call, link to the agreed-upon path (mentorship / mastermind waitlist / follow-up timing).

T+3 days - The Specific Resource

Subject: That [thing] I mentioned on the call

Body: Whatever specific resource Nick promised on the call (estimating template, sub relationship framework, podcast episode). Sent as clean PDF or shared Notion doc.

T+7 days - The Check-In

Subject: Did you run the estimating sheet yet?

Body: Soft accountability nudge. "Reply with a one-line update if you did. Reply with a question if you didn't."

T+14 days - The Decision

Subject: Where you landed

Body: Clean ask: "Are we moving forward with [agreed path] or holding off?" No pressure. Clear decision frame. Either route triggers the right next sequence.

Subject Line Bank (ongoing rotation)

For broadcasts beyond the welcome sequence:

  • "What I'd run if I were starting NS Builders from scratch"
  • "Three numbers I read before every site visit"
  • "The Friday-afternoon rule on a job site"
  • "Why I fired my best sub - and hired him back"
  • "Modern Craftsman is a standard before it's a brand"
  • "The client conversation most GCs avoid"
  • "What the project manager I hired in year three taught me"

Triggers + Tags

  • Tag by score tier on quiz completion
  • Tag by purchase (Mentorship $3K / $5K / mastermind application)
  • Tag by content engagement (opened 5+ in 30 days = "warm operator")
  • Auto-suppress promotional during active project launch periods
  • Re-engagement campaign at 60 days of inactivity
