237K Builders Watch the Process, Then Have Nowhere to Buy In

@nsbuilders has 237,000 followers watching you document the NS Builders process - job-site walkthroughs, custom home design, project management, material selection. They trust the craft. They want to build the way you build. And right now there is no product they can buy to get closer to that. The audience watches, nods, and scrolls on. Every week without a product is a warm audience decaying toward someone else's mentorship.

Cost of Waiting

~$15-50K/mo in mentorship revenue going to nobody. That compounds to ~$150-600K/yr in unbooked revenue while your content audience stays warm but un-monetised. A $1M+ custom builder following your content for six months is ready to buy. Right now there is nothing to buy. Each 30 days that pass = direct revenue never collected, not deferred.

6 months from now ·~$75-300K never collected. Plus 10,000+ new followers who followed, consumed, trusted - and then found another builder-educator who had a mentorship ready.

Fix · The Custom Builder Mentorship translating the NS Builders process into installable systems for $1M-$5M operators. Year-1 floor $150-400K at 50-120 mentees.

02

237K Followers, Zero Email Capture - No Asset From All That Trust

There is no scorecard, no quiz, no opt-in, no PDF visible across nsbuilders.com, the IG bio, or the YouTube channel. 237K followers generate reach - but zero compounding asset, because there is no email list. Every builder who taps away from your content is gone forever. Meanwhile builder-educators who set up the lead-capture you didn't are building lists that compound year after year off a fraction of your audience size.

Cost of Waiting

~$5-15K/mo in nurture-driven mentorship + cohort sales not happening - ~$50-150K/yr compounding for life of the list you don't have. 8-12% IG-to-email conversion with the right quiz = 19,000+ email subscribers waiting inside your existing audience right now. They're not on a list because there's no opt-in.

6 months from now ·~$25-75K never captured + an extra 5,000-10,000 warm followers permanently gone to builder-educators who set up the scorecard you didn't.

Fix · "The Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard" quiz - 8 questions, returns a 0-100 score, captures email, segments by build volume and biggest ops bottleneck. Less than 7 day install. $50-150K Year-1 lift, compounds for life of the list.
Compound Pickup
03

Modern Craftsman Podcast Has No Paid Tier - Premium Attention, Zero Revenue

Modern Craftsman is one of the most trusted podcasts in custom residential construction. Listeners spend 30-60 minutes per episode with your thinking - that is the highest-quality attention in the entire builder-education space. And right now the podcast generates zero direct revenue beyond brand equity. The podcast listener is the most ready-to-buy segment of your audience. There is no paid product attached to them.

Cost of Waiting

~$8-17K/mo in mastermind revenue never collected - the $100-200K/yr ARR layer keeps not getting built. The podcast listener who binged three episodes this week is not being routed to a product. Each episode published without a paid-tier CTA is reach without revenue.

6 months from now ·Six more months of premium podcast attention with no conversion event. Hundreds of $1M+ custom builders who spent 30+ hours with your content and had no product to graduate into.

Fix · "Modern Craftsman Mastermind" - $2,500-$5,000/yr group cohort, 40-person cap, quarterly live sessions with Nick. $100-200K/yr ARR from the highest-trust segment of the audience. Less than 7 day install + 60-day fill.
