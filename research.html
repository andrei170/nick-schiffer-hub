Funnel state

nsbuilders.com = company site for the construction business. CTA is oriented toward custom home project inquiries, not education or coaching. Not a funnel for builders wanting to learn.

@nsbuilders IG = builder-education content. Reels on job-site process, custom home design, material selection, project management. Strong engagement. No link-in-bio routing to any educational product, scorecard, or email capture.

YouTube @NSBuilders = long-form content: project walkthroughs, process videos, trade interviews. No call to action beyond subscribe. No mentorship landing page, no lead magnet, no opt-in sequence attached.

Modern Craftsman podcast = strong trade identity vehicle. No paid tier, no membership, no cohort attached to it.

No quiz, no audit, no scorecard visible. The 237K IG audience and the podcast listenership are generating awareness and trust for Nick's brand - but the conversion path from "follows @nsbuilders" to "pays Nick anything" does not exist for custom builders outside of Eastern MA.

Pitch leverage

  • 237K @nsbuilders IG + YouTube presence + Modern Craftsman podcast = three-channel warm audience with zero monetised off-ramp
  • Eastern MA luxury GC credibility since 2014 (decade of actual builds behind the content)
  • Podcast format = warm relationship with builder audience at scale (listeners feel they know Nick)
  • NS Builders team and process = proven system that can be productised for other custom builders
  • No existing mentorship competitor in the exact nick (luxury custom builder operator, podcast-led brand, Eastern MA roots) - the positioning is uncrowded

Wedge

Productise the NS Builders process into a mid-tier coaching offer for other custom builders:

  1. Free lead magnet - "The Custom Builder's Operations Scorecard" (quiz that scores a custom builder's business systems 0-100, segments by stage)
  2. Mid-tier: $3K-$5K Custom Builder Mentorship (the NS Builders process translated into installable systems for operators at $1M-$5M in custom residential work, 50-120 mentees)
  3. High-tier (new): $2K-$5K group coaching cohort for serious operators, Modern Craftsman-branded
  4. Email nurture + segmentation off the scorecard, routing by tier

Estimated lift: $300K-$800K/yr incremental Year-1 floor (mentorship + email-driven cohort fill), new revenue on top of NS Builders project income.

Sensitivities

  • Trade-first brand: Pitch must respect the craft-led, non-hype ethos. No "guru" framing, no "make millions" language.
  • NS Builders is the credibility anchor: Don't suggest replacing or deprioritising the construction business. The mentorship sits alongside it, not instead of it.
  • Modern Craftsman podcast is the trust vehicle: Any productised offer should feel like a natural extension of the podcast, not a departure.
  • Luxury positioning: The audience skews toward quality-led operators, not volume builders. Pricing and framing must respect that.

Pitch one-liner

"Nick, you've built the Modern Craftsman audience - 237K @nsbuilders, the podcast, a decade of Eastern MA luxury builds. Three bottlenecks I found inside the funnel are leaving the entire productised layer for other custom builders unbuilt. All install in less than 7 days. Want to see them?"
