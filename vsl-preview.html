Phase 1

VSL Preview Script - Nick Schiffer

Phase 1 deliverable · Short preview only - structure, cold open, and page placement notes for the Custom Builder's Playbook hero VSL.

Format

4-6 minute short preview VSL, embedded in the Custom Builder's Playbook landing-page hero above the fold. Not a full 18-minute explainer - this is the hook that earns the scroll. The preview establishes credibility, names the problem, and routes the viewer to apply. A longer full version films after the launch cohort confirms demand.

Cold Open (00:00 - 00:45)

On camera, Nick, job site or office setup:

"I've been building custom homes in Eastern Massachusetts since 2014. And every builder I talk to runs into the same three problems - usually in the same order. First the margin leaks. Then the sub bench gets unreliable. Then the Fridays stop being strategy and start being damage control. I rebuilt the NS Builders process three times before I figured out the system that makes those problems stop recurring. This course is that system - documented and installable."

The Stakes (00:45 - 02:00)

What the $1M-$5M custom builder is dealing with:

  • Estimating is inconsistent - margin varies job to job, and most of it isn't tracked
  • Sub relationships are transactional - good framers leave for the next GC who pays faster
  • Project management is reactive - Fridays are firefighting, not planning
  • The business is built around the owner's presence - nothing runs without them in the room
  • The brand is the quality of the work, not a system that scales

Specific number: a $2M custom build company leaking 12% on estimating inconsistencies leaves $240K on the table per year. The Custom Builder's Playbook closes that leak.

Who I Am (02:00 - 03:00)

Quick-hit credentials, no chronology:

  • Eastern MA luxury GC since 2014
  • Founder, NS Builders - custom residential, high-end residential, Eastern Massachusetts
  • @nsbuilders 237K - Modern Craftsman content brand
  • Host, Modern Craftsman podcast
  • The NS Builders process is documented and repeatable across a full team

The point is not the credentials. The point is that the system behind the content is installable.

What the Course Covers (03:00 - 04:30)

Six modules, one minute:

  1. Client Systems - the intake process that qualifies buyers before plans pull
  2. Estimating for Margin - the cost-build sheet that protects margin on every bid
  3. Project Management Without Firefighting - the Monday cadence that holds a build on schedule
  4. Sub Relationships That Hold - the retention structure that keeps quality subs on your bench
  5. Modern Craftsman Positioning - how to price and present work so clients stop shopping on price
  6. Scale or Stay - the decision framework for where to take the business next

Each module: async video + installable template + monthly Q&A with Nick.

The Close (04:30 - 05:30)

"If you've been watching the @nsbuilders content and thinking 'I want to build the way they build' - this is how. The application form is below. Four minutes to fill in. We open cohorts quarterly and cap at 100 to keep the live Q&A useful. The first ops win usually happens inside week one of module one."

Production Notes

  • Filming location: NS Builders office or active job site. Job site preferred for Modern Craftsman aesthetic - shows the real work, not a talking head in a studio.
  • Wardrobe: NS Builders branded workwear. No suit, no casual - the builder's working uniform.
  • Camera: Sony FX3 or equivalent. Natural light on job site; key + fill for office setup.
  • Pace: 1.0x watchable. No long pauses. B-roll of the build cuts every 20-30 seconds.
  • Music: none under talking head. Light ambient bed under B-roll only.
  • Captions: burned-in for IG and YouTube shorts; SRT for course platform.
  • Thumbnail poster: Nick on a job site, looking at camera, with a finished custom home in the background. Overlay: "Build a custom home business that doesn't eat your weekends."
